درباره ما

About Mehregan Physiotherapy

Established in 2006, Mehregan Physiotherapy Clinic has been serving patients in Chamgordan, Isfahan, with professional physiotherapy and rehabilitation services. Founded and led by Masoumeh Aslani, the clinic is equipped with modern facilities to help patients regain health, strength, and mobility.

Services

  • Six electrotherapy cabins
  • Mechanotherapy facilities
  • High-power laser therapy
  • Ultrasound therapy
  • Dry needling
  • Graston technique
